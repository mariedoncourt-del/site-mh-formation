# Design Direction — MH Formation v2

## Brand
- **Name**: MH FORMATION
- **Tagline**: Formation professionnelle continue
- **Location**: 20 Place Edmond Canet, 81000 Albi
- **SIRET**: 939 035 721 00017

## Colors
- **Primary (Bleu nuit)**: `#0E2340`
- **Background (Blanc chaud)**: `#F7F5F2`
- **Surface (Gris clair)**: `#D8D3CC`
- **Text muted**: `#6B7280`
- **Border**: `#E5E0D9`
- No black pure, no blue electric, no gradients

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond — light weight, very large, never bold
- **Body**: Inter — 18-20px desktop, generous line-height (1.7+)
- **Hierarchy**: Huge titles, calm body, lots of air

## Layout
- Max content width: 1180px
- Side margins: 90px desktop, 24px mobile
- Vertical spacing: 160px+ between sections desktop, 90-120px mobile
- Left-aligned (almost everything)
- Never more than 2 strong columns
- One idea per screen

## Components
- Separators: very thin, warm gray
- Lists: thin dashes (—), no bullets, no icons
- Buttons: outline only, transparent bg, subtle hover
- Links: light underline on hover
- Transitions: 0.4s ease, calm, never snappy

## Images
- Very few, high quality
- Natural light, wide framing, details
- Slight desaturation, soft contrast
- No stock corporate, no fake teams, no startup offices

## Animations
- Fade-in only on scroll
- No parallax, no zoom, no SaaS animations
- Max 0.4s duration, ease curve

## Anti-patterns (FORBIDDEN)
- Pure black, electric blue, gradients
- Heavy shadows, multiple colors
- Stock corporate imagery
- Cards with shadows (SaaS style)
- Pictograms, colored icons, checkmarks
- Centering everything
- Bold condensed titles
- Parallax, autoplay videos, sliders
